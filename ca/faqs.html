Frequently Asked Questions

Questions Answers
Are red pandas related to giant pandas? No, red pandas are in the family Ailuridae, while giant pandas are in the family Ursidae
What are red pandas closest relative? They are apart of a superfamily which contains weasels and raccoons
How long do red pandas live? Up to 15 years
How can I help preserve red pandas? Donate to wildlife conservation foundations or adopt a red panda from one!
Are red pandas friendly? They can be very friendly. However they are also very territorial and will strike with their sharp claws if threatened
Do red pandas hibernate? No, but they become much more dormant and are only active when eating
Are red pandas herbivores? No, red pandas are actually omnivores, though their diet is 98% bamboo!
